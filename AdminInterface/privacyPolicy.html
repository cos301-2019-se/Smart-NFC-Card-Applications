Privacy Policy of Link

Link operates the https://smart-nfc-application.herokuapp.com/ website, as well as the Link mobile application which provides the service of enabling users to use NFC enabled smart phones to share business cards and visitor packages.

This page is used to inform website visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service, the Link website and/or the Link mobile application.

If you choose to use our Service, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation with this policy. The Personal Information that we collect are used for providing and improving the Service. We will not use or share your information with anyone.

Information Collection and Use

For the purpose of using the Link mobile application, if you are not registered in the Link system, we will only store your UUID, however we do not use this outside of verification.

For the purpose of using the Link mobile application, if you are registered in the Link system (which is done by your company), we require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, phone number, and email address. The information that we collect will be used to contact or identify you.

For the purpose of using the entire Link serivce, if you -the company- applying to use the Link services, will be required to provide a website link and a company name

Log Data

We are happy to inform you that we do not log any data from you other than information provided when registering.

Cookies

Cookies are files with small amount of data that is commonly used as anonymous unique identifier. These are sent to your browser from our website that you visit and are stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Our website uses these "cookies" to store information and to improve your experience.
